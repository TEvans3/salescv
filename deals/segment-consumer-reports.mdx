## How It Started

AE-led outbound messaging which leveraged hiring signals to pinpoint an initial Champion, CR's Sr. Growth Product Manager, who had used Segment at three prior companies and had been brought into CR's core product team recently. After initial conversations in mid-2024, we had identified a clear gap in CR's stack: no real-time data router, no unified customer profile, and a 7-day delay getting data into downstream tools. I had to work with this Champion from the bottom-up to build the internal business case and navigate the org to sell the vision of modernizing their Customer Data infrastructure to power product and marketing personalization.

## MEDDPICC

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="M — Metrics">
    The business case was built around CR's 5M+ member base and four core KPIs:
    - **LTV**: Higher conversion and lower churn via real-time personalization.
    - **Engagement**: Goal of increasing MAU and visit frequency via **cross-device identity resolution** (Success criteria: >95% accuracy).
    - **Retention ROI**: 1% churn reduction on 5M members = **$2M in annual retained revenue**.
    - **Performance**: Validated **sub-300ms** Profile API latency for homepage personalization and **sub-60s** trait sync to Braze.
    - **Operational ROI**: Eliminated ~10 manual engineering hours per week spent on DWH-to-Monetate scripts and custom ID-stitching logic.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="E — Economic Buyer">
    **CDO**. 
      - Converted a stalled 2-year enterprise agreement by architecting a 3-month gated pilot structured around technical success milestones. Mitigated "Permanent CTO" vacancy risk by leveraging a Solutions Architect to build production-ready use cases ahead of schedule. Delivered an on-site executive demo that validated all exit criteria prior to kick-off, ensuring 100% pilot-to-contract conversion and protecting multi-year ARR.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="D — Decision Criteria">
    - **Real-time event collection** (Web, iOS, Android, Server-side).
    - **Schema enforcement** across multiple sources.

    - **Reverse ETL** from Databricks for profile traits.
    - **Profile API** for < 5ms site personalization.

## Technical Validation

Personally led a full-day onsite workshop at HQ in Yonkers for 10+ stakeholders. The following POC validated:
- **ID Resolution**: Stitched anonymous and member IDs across all CR systems.
- **Reverse ETL**: Pulled profile traits from Databricks into Segment Profiles.
- **Real-time Personalization**: Profile API used to serve dynamic homepage themes, product experiences, and marketing communication based on computed traits.
- **Mobile Activation**: Triggered personalized push notifications via Braze.

## Negotiation Tactics

**Gated Pilot Strategy:** Recognizing a leadership vacuum (vacant CTO) and internal skepticism, I architected a Paid 3-Month Gated Pilot with rigorous, pre-defined technical exit criteria. This transformed a "high-risk" multi-year commitment into a "low-risk" validation phase. By front-loading the value via a Solutions Architect before the pilot even began, I made the eventual transition to the $523k contract a technical formality rather than a commercial hurdle.

---

## Competitive Differentiation

**Ecosystem Co-operation:** Rather than fighting the Data Office’s "warehouse-first" mandate, I brought in the Databricks Partner Team to co-present. We demonstrated how Segment acts as a "Workload Multiplier" for their existing DWH investment. This neutralized the "Build In-House" detractor by aligning Segment with the CDO’s long-term infrastructure roadmap. It turned a potential competitor into a strategic ally, ensuring the solution was seen as "Warehouse-Native" rather than a data silo.
