## How It Started

Leveraging a prior "Closed Lost" relationship from 2022, I re-initiated the cycle by multi-threading into the new VP of Customer Experience and Director of Global Marketing. Through deep discovery, we identified that Roland's marketing was dangerously reliant on third-party cookies, posing a risk to their 3-year digital transformation roadmap. I reframed the conversation from "Data Collection" to "Data Ownership," positioning Segment as the only platform capable of stitching legacy hardware purchase data with real-time Roland Cloud behavior to drive personalized music-creator experiences.

## Negotiation Tactics

**The "White-Glove" Pivot:** When Roland expressed concern about implementation complexity and internal resource limits, I didn't just sell software. I brought in a specialized Professional Services partner (Stitch) and bundled their support into a comprehensive transformation plan. I reframed the additional $100K in service fees as "Migration Insurance," ensuring the CEO that the $612k platform investment would be fully functional and delivering value by H2 2024.
