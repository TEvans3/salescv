## How It Started

Outbound engagement targeted the CEO and Founder by leveraging market signals regarding iPlum's struggle with 10DLC campaign vetting and deliverability on the incumbent (Bandwidth) stack. I positioned Twilio not just as a carrier, but as a compliance-first orchestration layer.
