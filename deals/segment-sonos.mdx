## The Strategic Motion

The evaluation was triggered by a "Warehouse-First" mandate. Sonos was migrating to **Snowflake + dbt** as their central data lake but lacked a real-time ingestion and identity layer. I positioned Segment as the "Workload Multiplier" for Snowflake, ensuring their high-value data science models could actually be activated in real-time across **Braze**, **Salesforce Marketing Cloud**, and their **Global App ecosystem**.

## Competitive Differentiation

**The "Reseller Trap":** The biggest threat was Chord Commerce (a legacy reseller). I discovered they were running on an older version of Segment. I successfully argued for "Direct Ownership," proving that Sonos deserved a direct relationship with the Product and Engineering teams for a mission-critical platform, especially given their scale of 14M households.
