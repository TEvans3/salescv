**TLDR:** Won a three-way competitive RFP against Salesforce and a legacy reseller (Chord) by building a \$7M value-at-stake model using Sonos' own operational data. By reframing Segment as a "Warehouse-Native" accelerator for Snowflake rather than a competitor to it, we cleared the CIO's primary technical hurdle. ## How It Started

AE-led outbound leveraged hiring signals and existing \$3K Twilio infrastructure spend to identify Sonos as a high-fit target. Discovery revealed a fragmented stack (Salesforce Marketing/Commerce Cloud + Snowflake) with no unified profile or real-time routing layer. I structured the discovery as a co-investment, gaining access to the Director of Data Insights and the CIO by committing to build an auditable value model using Sonos' specific conversion and AOV figures rather than industry benchmarks.

## Negotiation Tactics

**Value Anchoring:** By validating the \$7M benefit with the CIO first, the \$656K price point was viewed as a 10:1 ROI rather than a cost.
**ARR Protection:** Bundled Business Critical Services (BCS) and Premium Support into the total contract, allowing procurement to see one "package" while keeping the underlying software ACV healthy and undiscounted.
