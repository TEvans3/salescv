## How It Started

Leveraging a "Challenger" approach, I initiated outbound engagement targeting the CTO by highlighting the massive "Cost of Inaction" associated with their current dual-track struggle: maintaining an expensive legacy Jive Communications stack while simultaneously failing to build a custom VoIP solution in-house. Through technical discovery, I identified that their internal project was stalled due to the complexity of multi-tenant orchestration. I reframed the conversation from "buying a vendor" to "partnering with a platform" that mirrors the CTO’s preference for open-source flexibility while providing enterprise-grade reliability.
