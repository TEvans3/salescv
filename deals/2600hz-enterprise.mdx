## How It Started

The engagement began as a high-velocity 6-week cycle from cold outreach to close. RevenueWell was at a critical crossroads: they were struggling to maintain two disparate VoIP platforms (including Jive Communications) while simultaneously failing to build a custom in-house solution. I identified the CTO as the primary entry point, leveraging his existing affinity for the Open-Source community. By aligning 2600Hz as a "Partner" rather than just a "Vendor," we demonstrated how they could sunset their expensive legacy tools and launch a superior product in weeks rather than the 6–12 months they had originally forecasted for an internal build.
