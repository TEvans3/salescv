## About This Book

This is a curated set of enterprise deal case studies documenting sales cycles I've owned from source to close across **2600Hz**, **Twilio Segment**, and **Twilio Strategic**. Each case study details the full stakeholder landscape, MEDDPICC methodology, competitive differentiation, and negotiation strategy behind deals ranging from mid-six figures to $1.2M ARR.

I built this to illustrate not just what I've closed, but *how* I sell — the frameworks, judgment calls, and relationship work that move complex enterprise deals forward.

## My Background

I've spent 8+ years selling enterprise SaaS across communications infrastructure, customer data platforms, and developer tooling. I specialize in technical enterprise deals: multi-stakeholder buying committees, InfoSec reviews, API-forward products, and founder-led companies scaling to enterprise.
